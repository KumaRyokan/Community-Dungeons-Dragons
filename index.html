To start off with let's let everyone know the world that we are exploring currently. Welcome to Kriios
An island continent with 5 main regions Grackman hold, being the northernmost one, home of foul Gillins, terrifying kracken like creatures that can swallow up kingdoms from the cracking ice, and the birthplace of Grackmans. harsh cold bites through your very flesh, but as a Grackman, the cold embraces you like a comforting blanket. The great frozen river being the next one to follow, less cold, but more of an arctic wasteland, the terrain is mostly flat, except for the odd piece of terrain sticking out, the ground is slippery, as it is mostly made from thick sheets of ice, about a meter below however, the Gillins lie in wait, begging for you to stop for just a moment, so they may take your flesh to the bottom of its home.
Next up are the Wildlands, a common ground for everyone, lush trees and beautiful lakes surrounded in its four seasons, neither of which are boring. the people here are happy, and quite flush with diversity, from gnomes to high elves, those who managed to hear of this elusive island tend to spend most of their time here, as the northernmost and southern most regions are uninhabitable from commonfolk. Below that are the Dunes, great deserts of scorching heat, and hunting ground for the Sarraques, gargantuan 100m wide fangworms that eat through the sand with utmost elegance, they prey on bands of adventurers who foolishly decide to make camp for the night, however there are locations within the dunes that are inaccessible to the great Arraques, harder bits of crusted stone, lurching forwards for miles where people decided to take up residence, some areas have their own ecosystem, and serves as a tropical, summerful paradise. The southernmost region, home to the Balions and scorching heat, is aptly named the Scorchlands. common temperature ranges from 50-60 degrees with highest temperatures recorded at 70. this land is caked with a dry, sandstone surface, cracked and desolate, its land does not bear trees, or even such resembling. the only fuel source around that any Balions holds have for cooking meat, is the harsh sun, or the ruins, scatterred from an ancient civilization, these ruins contained not many riches, but the most valuable resource of all for them. wood, coal, and other burnable substances.
Each city has its own supplies and demands, with that, a crate of apples, while only 10g in Kelinan (wildlands) could be worth 100g in a city belonging to the dunes. and as such, some cities may not even have a value for currency, with a minded goal on helping each other survive in their community. However their services are still available to outsiders, but at trade value. i.e Crate of apples= nonmagical longsword None of the regions are particularly at war, as this is an island of peace and tourists, fortunately the residents of this island realized far sooner than the rest of the world that life is far too short for petty squabbles over land and such, and each are quite happy with the land they control. Power hungry leaders and tyrants are often dealt with in a timely manner by their people. After all. just because 2 Kriians don't like each other, doesn't mean they can make others not like each other.
